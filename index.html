Academic Q&A Platform

Our Stack Overflow-inspired Q&A platform is the heart of Mowquery, designed specifically for academic collaboration and knowledge sharing. Here's what makes it special:

Question Posting & Management

Students can post detailed academic questions with rich text formatting, code snippets, and mathematical equations. Questions can be tagged with relevant subjects, topics, and courses for easy discovery. The platform supports multiple file attachments, including images and PDFs, to provide context and clarity.

Answer System

Answers can be upvoted by the community, with the best solutions rising to the top. Students can mark answers as "accepted" when they solve their problem. All answers support rich formatting, including LaTeX for mathematical notation, code highlighting, and image embedding.

Tagging & Search

A comprehensive tagging system allows questions to be categorized by subject, topic, difficulty level, and institution. Advanced search functionality enables students to filter by these tags, search within specific time periods, and sort by various metrics including vote count and answer count.

Reputation & Rewards

Contributors earn reputation points for quality answers, helpful edits, and insightful questions. Higher reputation unlocks additional platform privileges, such as moderation tools and the ability to create new tags.
